National Defence College, Nigeria

Millitary Training Institution for the Nigerian Armed Forces

Brigadier General Edward Adeyemi Koleoso started his military career in 1993 at Nigerian Defence Academy as a member of 45th Regular Course. Edward graduated from the Academy in 1998 with a Bachelors of Engineering degree in Electrical Electronic and was commissioned into the Nigerian Army Electrical and Mechanical Engineers (NAEME) Corps as a Second Lieutenant. During the course of his career, Edward has attended several military courses some of which include; Armoured Fighting Vehicle Course, Officers’ Armoured and Instrument Course, EME Young Officers’, Company Commanders’ and Commanding Officers’ Courses all at Nigerian Army School of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering (NASEME) Auchi, Nigeria. He also had his Junior and Senior Staff Courses at Armed Forces Command and Staff College – Jaji, Nigeria as well as his Higher Defence and Strategic Course at the National Defence College, Nigeria. Furthermore, he holds a Post Graduate Diploma Certificate in Business Management from Kaduna Polytechnic and a Masters of Science Certificate in Gun Systems Design from Cranfield University, United Kingdom. He is a member of Nigerian Society of Engineers, Council for the Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria and an alumnus of Harvard University, United States of America where he did his Senior Executive Fellows Course. Brigadier General Koleoso has served the Country in different capacity during his military career, some of the appoints held are; Officer Commanding Armament and Radar Department in Artillery Base Workshop, Military Assistant to Director General Defence Industries Corporation of Nigeria, Commanding Officer of 910 Division Workshop, Commanding Officer of 1012 Brigade Workshop and Commanding Officer of Guards Brigade Workshop. Others include Acting Deputy Commandant and Director of Studies at Nigerian Army School of Electrical and Mechanical Engineering, Acting Director of Equipment Services and Weapon Engineering both at Headquarters NAEME as well as Acting Managing Director Nigerian Army Special Vehicle Plant amongst others. He is a thorough breed engineer with great affinity for research and development, and a proud recipient of the Nigerian Army Invention Medal. He had a sting of operational experience in Darfur as a member of African Union Mission in Sudan. Brigadier General Edward Adeyemi Koleoso enjoys playing Squash, Jogging and listening to classical music.
